Clearmatics - Mission

Our mission is to build peer-to-peer infrastructure for a machine-driven future that is resistant to the monopoly-making tendencies of network effects inherent in today's client-server architectures.We call our technology approach "decentralised automation", which combines cryptography, consensus protocols and economic mechanism design. We are designing, building and deploying p2p platforms for market participants to transact seemlessly and securely without unnecessary intermediaries.
